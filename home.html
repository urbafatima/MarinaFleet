Buy a Yacht

Buying a superyacht is one of life’s pinnacle achievements. However, the challenge of finding the perfect yacht at the right price is not a journey to embark on lightly or without an experienced hand to guide you.

Luxury Yacht Sales

Marina Fleet offers the largest fleet of superyachts for sale in the world which features a broad range of yachts including luxury motor yachts, classic sailing yachts, super yachts and mega yachts. We also have access to thousands more, including those not publicly listed for sale. We work hand-in-hand with our clients to guide and advise them on all aspects of the purchase process. You can search for yachts using the specialised search feature above or you can contact us to let us know your precise requirements. Once we have a clear understanding of your requirements we will hand-pick a selection of the most suitable yachts for you to review and organise visits on board those short listed and then guide you.

Wealth Of Expertise

Selling or purchasing a large yacht demands professional expertise and extensive market knowledge and Fraser has been a world leader in new and brokerage yacht sales for over 70 years. Focusing on the sale and purchase of yachts over 30m, our large team of highly experienced brokers visits hundreds of yachts every year and the world’s most important shipyards. We are in regular contact with hundreds of yacht owners and potential buyers, providing them with an in-depth understanding of the market place, while our advanced database allows us to bring buyers and sellers together with greater efficiency. Whether you are looking for a new build or a previously owned yacht.
