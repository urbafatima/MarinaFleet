The esteemed Seagull II yacht represents the pinnacle of luxury and sophistication on the high seas. Built in 1952 by the renowned Uljanik Shipyard in Croatia, this vessel has been meticulously refurbished to combine its classic charm with modern amenities, ensuring an unparalleled yachting experience.

Capable of accommodating up to 12 guests, Seagull II features 7 exquisitely appointed cabins, including a grand master suite and opulent VIP staterooms, all designed to offer the utmost in comfort and elegance. The interior boasts spacious living and dining areas adorned with tasteful décor, while state-of-the-art entertainment systems provide ample options for leisure and enjoyment.

Guests are attended to by a dedicated crew of 9 professionals, each committed to delivering the highest standards of service. Whether it’s preparing gourmet meals, organizing activities, or attending to personal requests, the crew ensures that every need is met with the utmost care and attention.

The expansive outdoor decks of Seagull II are perfect for relaxation and socializing, featuring luxurious amenities such as a jacuzzi. For those seeking adventure, the yacht is equipped with a variety of water toys, including jet skis, snorkeling gear, and kayaks, providing endless opportunities for fun and exploration.

Seagull II is available for charter and is ideal for exploring the stunning coastlines of the Mediterranean, including popular destinations such as the French Riviera, the Italian coast, and the idyllic Greek islands. This splendid yacht seamlessly blends timeless elegance with modern luxury, offering an unforgettable and distinguished maritime journey for all who embark on her.
