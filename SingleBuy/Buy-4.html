ABOUT ICE YACTH

The Ice is a magnificent and luxurious superyacht, crafted by the renowned Lürssen shipyard in Germany and launched in 2005. Designed for elegance and performance, Ice can accommodate up to 14 guests in 7 spacious and beautifully appointed cabins, including a master suite and VIP cabins. A dedicated crew of 27 professionals ensures exceptional service and a seamless experience. The yacht's interior features luxurious living areas, a formal dining room, and state-of-the-art entertainment systems, as well as a wellness area with a fully equipped gym, massage room, sauna, and steam room. Expansive outdoor decks provide ample space for relaxation and entertainment, with a jacuzzi, sunbathing loungers, and al fresco dining options. For water sports enthusiasts, Ice offers a variety of water toys, including jet skis, diving equipment, and snorkeling gear, along with tenders for easy shore excursions. With a cruising speed of 15 knots and a top speed of 18 knots, Ice is ideal for long-distance voyages to destinations such as the Mediterranean and the Caribbean.

The Ice is a magnificent and luxurious superyacht, designed for both elegance and performance. Built in 2005 by the renowned Lürssen shipyard in Germany, Ice is a masterpiece of engineering and design, offering unparalleled comfort and sophistication.

Ice can accommodate up to 14 guests in 7 spacious and beautifully appointed cabins, including a luxurious master suite, VIP cabins, and guest cabins. Each cabin is designed with elegance and comfort in mind, ensuring a restful and private experience for all guests.

A dedicated crew of 27 professionals provides exceptional service, attending to every need with meticulous attention to detail. The crew's expertise ensures a seamless and enjoyable experience on board.

The interior of Ice is exquisitely designed with luxurious living areas, a formal dining room, and state-of-the-art entertainment systems, including high-definition TVs, music systems, and Wi-Fi. The yacht also features a wellness area with a fully equipped gym, massage room, sauna, and steam room, providing guests with the ultimate relaxation and rejuvenation experience.

Ice boasts expansive outdoor decks that are perfect for relaxation and entertainment. Guests can enjoy the jacuzzi, sunbathe on comfortable loungers, or dine al fresco while taking in breathtaking sea views. The outdoor areas are designed to offer both comfort and luxury.

For those who enjoy water sports, Ice is equipped with a variety of water toys, including jet skis, diving equipment, and snorkeling gear. The yacht also includes tenders for easy and comfortable shore excursions, allowing guests to explore nearby areas with ease.

Ice is powered by advanced engineering that ensures a smooth and stable ride. It has a cruising speed of 15 knots (about 17 mph) and can reach a top speed of 18 knots (about 21 mph). With a substantial range, Ice is ideal for long-distance voyages and exploring diverse destinations.

Available for charter, Ice is perfect for exploring stunning destinations around the world. Whether it's the Mediterranean, the Caribbean, or any other exotic location, Ice offers an unforgettable yachting experience with its blend of luxury, comfort, and adventure.
