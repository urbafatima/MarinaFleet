The Moonlight II yacht is a stunning example of luxury and elegance, designed to offer an extraordinary yachting experience. Built by the renowned Neorion shipyard in 2005 and refitted in 2015, Moonlight II is a 91.4-meter (300-foot) superyacht that combines sophisticated design with modern amenities.

Moonlight II can accommodate up to 36 guests in 18 beautifully designed cabins, including a luxurious master suite, VIP staterooms, and additional guest cabins. The interiors are elegantly appointed with high-quality materials and feature spacious living areas, a formal dining room, and state-of-the-art entertainment systems. Each cabin offers a serene and private retreat, ensuring maximum comfort and relaxation for all guests.

A professional crew of 36 members ensures that every need is met with exceptional service and attention to detail. From expert navigation to gourmet meals prepared by a talented chef, the crew is dedicated to providing an outstanding yachting experience.

The expansive decks of Moonlight II offer numerous spaces for relaxation and entertainment. Guests can enjoy the sunbathing areas, unwind in the jacuzzi, or dine al fresco while taking in breathtaking sea views. For those seeking adventure, the yacht is equipped with a variety of water toys, including jet skis, kayaks, diving equipment, and snorkeling gear. The yacht also features a helipad for easy access to and from remote locations.

Moonlight II boasts a range of wellness and leisure facilities, including a spa, sauna, gym, and a cinema room, ensuring guests can relax and stay entertained throughout their voyage.
