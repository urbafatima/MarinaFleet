ABOUT YERSIN

The Yersin is an extraordinary and versatile explorer yacht, meticulously crafted by the renowned Piriou Shipyard in France and launched in 2015. This exceptional vessel is designed for those seeking both adventure and luxury on the high seas. With a robust and innovative design, Yersin is capable of embarking on long voyages to some of the world's most remote and fascinating destinations.

Yersin can comfortably accommodate up to 12 guests in 8 beautifully appointed cabins, including a master suite, VIP cabins, and additional guest cabins. Each cabin is elegantly designed to provide the utmost comfort and luxury, ensuring a serene and private experience for all guests.

A professional crew of 24 members is dedicated to providing outstanding service, ensuring that every need is met with meticulous attention to detail. The crew's expertise and hospitality create an unforgettable experience for all on board.

The interior of Yersin features spacious and sophisticated living areas, with comfortable lounges, a formal dining room, and state-of-the-art entertainment systems. The yacht also boasts a wellness area, including a massage room, gym, sauna, and steam room, allowing guests to relax and rejuvenate in style.

Yersin is equipped with research facilities, such as a laboratory and classroom, making it ideal for scientific expeditions and educational purposes. This unique feature sets Yersin apart as a vessel capable of supporting a wide range of missions and explorations.

The expansive outdoor decks offer ample space for relaxation and socializing. Guests can enjoy the jacuzzi, sunbathe on the comfortable loungers, or dine al fresco while taking in the stunning sea views. The decks are designed to provide both comfort and entertainment.

For those who love water sports and exploration, Yersin is equipped with a variety of water toys, including jet skis, diving equipment, and snorkeling gear. The yacht also includes tenders for easy and comfortable shore excursions, allowing guests to explore nearby areas with ease.

Yersin has a cruising speed of 11 knots (about 13 mph) and can reach a top speed of 15 knots (about 17 mph). With a range of over 12,000 nautical miles, Yersin is perfect for long-distance exploration and can reach destinations such as the Mediterranean, Caribbean, Arctic, and Antarctic regions.

Available for charter, Yersin is ideal for exploring a wide array of stunning and diverse locations around the globe. Whether it's the glamorous French Riviera, the picturesque Italian coast, the idyllic Greek islands, or the remote and pristine waters of the Arctic and Antarctic, Yersin offers an unparalleled maritime adventure.
